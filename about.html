Our Vision for Water Security

We are leveraging cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence to build a future where every community in India has access to safe, sustainable groundwater resources.

Our Mission

To empower farmers, communities, and government bodies with accurate, data-driven insights for water well placement. We aim to reduce the financial risk of failed drilling attempts and promote the sustainable management of India's vital groundwater.

Our Technology

AquaSight utilizes a suite of machine learning models trained to analyze complex geological patterns. Our AI processes vast datasets to provide clear, actionable predictions on:

  • Site Suitability
  • Expected Water Depth & Yield
  • Optimal Drilling Methods
  • Regional Water Quality

Data-Driven Insights

Our predictions are built upon a robust foundation of national data from the Central Ground Water Board (CGWB) under the NAQUIM programme, including:

  • Lithology & Geophysical Logs
  • Aquifer Maps
  • Water Level & Quality Data

Supporting National Goals

This project is proudly aligned with the goals of the Ministry of Jal Shakti and directly supports the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goal 6 (SDG 6): ensuring access to clean water and sanitation for all. By providing reliable intelligence, AquaSight contributes to a water-secure future for India.
